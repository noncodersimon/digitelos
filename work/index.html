Work

Real projects, real organisations

Every one of these is live, in use, and still looked after. Most are within a short drive of Slapton.

Save our Shoreline

Coastal charity · Slapton, Devon

The charity's campaigning had outgrown a website nobody on the committee could update.

A full website rebuild: news, campaigns and donations, with an editor the committee actually uses. Built on WordPress, so any trustee can publish an update in minutes.

The committee now runs the whole site themselves.

Slapton Village Hall - pickleball scheduler

Village hall · Slapton, Devon

Weekly pickleball sessions ran on paper, and the rotation arguments were becoming a fixture of their own.

A live rotation scheduler: fair turn-taking, shared live state, and it works on any phone brought to the hall. The hall's website came with an explicit design brief too - its colour scheme follows a painting that hangs in the hall itself.

Used every week. No more paper.

Totnes & Kingsbridge Catholic parishes

Three churches · South Devon

Three churches, one overworked set of coordinators, and no single place for Mass times, newsletters or volunteer rotas.

A complete website serving all three churches: Mass times, newsletters, donations, and interactive volunteer rotas the coordinators run themselves.

The rotas that used to live on paper now organise themselves.

totneskingsbridgerc.uk

Desktops for Peace

Volunteer project

A volunteer project supporting the peace initiatives of Abuna Elias Chacour, with no public home for what it does or way to take donations.

A simple, fast website: what the project does, news of events, and online donations - one-off or regular.

One link to share, wherever the project is mentioned.

desktopsforpeace.com
