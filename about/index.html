About

I'm Simon

I build and look after websites and digital tools for charities, clubs and small organisations across Devon.

I've spent over twenty years in professional IT service management, with broad experience of how technology is used in many different environments - large and small. The common thread has always been keeping important systems running properly: security, backups, accessibility, data protection. I'm ITIL-certified, which is the boring way of saying I take running things seriously.

Digitelos exists because small organisations deserve the same care. A village hall, a parish, a coastal charity - none of them can afford an agency, and none of them should have to gamble on getting a website that's abandoned the day after launch. So I build sites and tools properly, price them plainly, and stay around afterwards.

I live in Slapton, near Start Bay in South Devon. Most projects start with a friendly chat - in person if you're local, or by phone and email if you're further away. Either works just as well.
