Services

What we untangle

Six things we do well, for organisations of five to five hundred people — whether you're untangling years of accumulated IT or taking the first steps of your digital journey. Everything starts small, proves itself, then grows.

Service management

ITIL-based service management, deployed and transformed — incident, request, change and knowledge that fit the size of your team, not a textbook.

Websites that work

Design and build of fast, accessible sites — from charity one-pagers to full digital presences — with hosting, maintenance and care included.

Microsoft 365, properly used

AWS & cloud foundations

Process automation

Approach

How the untangling happens

  1. 01

    Discover

    We sit with the mess, without judgement. We map what's really there — systems, contracts, workarounds, and the people quietly holding it all together.

  2. 02

    Simplify

    We remove before we add. Fewer tools, clearer processes, one obvious way to do each thing — so the technology recedes and the work comes forward.

  3. 03

    Embed

    We leave you self-sufficient: documented, automated and calm — with fractional support on hand when you want a steady pair of eyes.

About

Technology should have a telos

Most IT problems aren't really technology problems — they're accumulations. Tools bought for reasons nobody remembers, processes shaped by old emergencies, licences quietly renewing. Digitelos exists to give all of it a purpose again, or to help you let it go.

We're an independent, vendor-neutral consultancy based in Devon, working with SMEs and charities across the UK and anywhere remote work reaches.

  • Remote-first anywhere else
  • Independent and vendor-neutral
