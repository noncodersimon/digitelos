Privacy

The short version

This site collects as little as possible, and nothing sneaky.

What this site does

No analytics, no tracking, no advertising cookies. The only thing stored in your browser is your light-or-dark theme choice, and it never leaves your device.

The contact form doesn't send anything to a server - it opens your own email app with the details filled in, so you can see exactly what you're sending before you send it.

Email

If you email me, I keep your message for as long as it takes to deal with your enquiry and any work that follows. I don't share it, sell it, or add you to a mailing list.

Hosting

The site is served by GitHub Pages, which logs basic technical data (like IP addresses) to run the service - see GitHub's privacy statement.
